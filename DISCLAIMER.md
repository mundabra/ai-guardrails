# Disclaimer

`@mundabra/ai-guardrails` is a lightweight guardrails library for AI applications.

It is designed to reduce some common classes of unsafe input and output. It is not a guarantee of safety, security, compliance, or correctness.

## Important Limits

This package does not guarantee:

- complete prompt injection prevention
- complete moderation coverage
- complete secret or sensitive-data detection
- factual correctness or hallucination prevention
- compliance with any legal, regulatory, or industry standard
- suitability for safety-critical, regulated, or high-risk deployments without additional controls

## Use At Your Own Risk

You are responsible for evaluating whether this package is appropriate for your application, threat model, users, and regulatory environment.

In particular, do not use this package as your only control for:

- production security boundaries
- authorization decisions
- medical, legal, financial, or other high-stakes advice systems
- regulated workloads that require formal assurance or certification

Nothing in this repository should be treated as legal, compliance, security, or risk-management advice.
